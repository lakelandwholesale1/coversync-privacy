Privacy Policy for CoverSync Live

Effective Date: July 2026

CoverSync Live ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application operates regarding data collection and usage.

1. Information Access & Usage

CoverSync Live requires access to the Android Notification Listener Service. This permission is used exclusively to detect media playback state and extract active album artwork and track metadata directly on your device.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or listening history. All metadata reading and wallpaper rendering occur locally on your smartphone. No information is sent to external servers or third parties.

3. In-App Purchases & Billing

CoverSync Live offers a paid monthly subscription processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not process or store financial payment details on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

CoverSync Live does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@lakelanddigital.com
